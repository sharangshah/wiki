# Philosophy

First principles, ethics, and meaning — the questions underneath all other questions.

This domain is the foundation layer. Every other domain implicitly relies on philosophical commitments about what counts as evidence, what matters, and how to live. This section makes those commitments explicit.

---

## Key Areas

Epistemology — how we know what we know, and the limits of knowledge. Ethics — frameworks for navigating trade-offs when there's no clean answer. Meaning — what constitutes a well-lived life, drawn from Stoic, Vedantic, and existentialist traditions. Philosophy of science — what separates robust knowledge from sophisticated speculation.
