# Cross-Domain

Where domains collide — the most interesting ideas live at intersections.

This is the synthesis layer. When a principle from Ayurveda illuminates a pattern in portfolio management, or a geopolitical shift changes the health landscape, the insight belongs here.

---

## Why This Section Exists

Specialists go deep. Generalists go wide. The real edge comes from going deep in multiple domains and then mapping the connections. This section is where that mapping happens.

---

## Key Themes

How cognitive biases from psychology interact with investment decision-making in wealth. Where Ayurvedic thinking about balance and cycles parallels macro-economic cycle theory. What philosophy of science says about the evidence standards we apply to health claims. How geopolitical power shifts create investment opportunities that pure financial analysis misses.
