# Welcome

This is my personal wiki — a living archive of research, thinking, and evolving ideas across the domains that matter to me.

Every page here is a **compiled view**, not a raw note. I feed sources (earnings transcripts, research papers, books, articles, conversations) into this system, and it distils them into structured, interlinked knowledge. When my thinking changes, the pages update — and the [Changelog](changelog.md) records what shifted and why.

---

## Domains

**[Wealth](wealth/index.md)** — Equity research, portfolio strategy, macro and markets. The analytical engine.

**[Health](health/index.md)** — Ayurveda, nutrition, fitness. Ancient wisdom cross-referenced with modern evidence.

**[Psychology](psychology/index.md)** — Mental models, behavioural patterns, decision-making. How the mind works and fails.

**[Geopolitics](geopolitics/index.md)** — Power dynamics, trade flows, civilisational patterns. The forces that shape everything else.

**[Philosophy](philosophy/index.md)** — First principles, ethics, meaning. The questions underneath all other questions.

**[Cross-Domain](cross-domain/index.md)** — Where domains collide. The most interesting ideas live at intersections.

---

## How This Works

This wiki follows the **"compilation over retrieval"** pattern. Rather than searching raw documents every time I have a question, knowledge is compiled once into structured pages and kept current as new information arrives.

Think of it as a personal Wikipedia — except every page reflects my specific analytical lens, and the version history shows how my thinking has evolved over time.
