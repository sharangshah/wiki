# Psychology

Mental models, behavioural patterns, and decision-making — how the mind works and how it fails.

This domain connects cognitive science, behavioural economics, and practical wisdom. The focus is on models that are actionable: not just "interesting to know" but "changes how I decide."

---

## Key Areas

Cognitive biases and how they compound in financial decision-making. Mental models that survive across domains. The psychology of risk, loss, and regret. Habit formation and identity-level change. Emotional regulation as a meta-skill.
