# Changelog

A record of how thinking has evolved — what changed, when, and why.

This page tracks significant updates across the wiki. Minor edits (typos, formatting) aren't logged here. What matters is when a **view shifts**, a **thesis updates**, or **new knowledge changes** an existing position.

---

## How to Read This

Each entry follows a consistent format:

- **Date** — When the update was made
- **Domain** — Which section of the wiki was affected
- **Page** — The specific page that changed
- **What changed** — A plain-language summary of the shift
- **Why** — What new information or thinking triggered the change
