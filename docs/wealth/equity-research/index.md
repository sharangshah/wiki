# Equity Research

Company-level deep dives — investment theses, due diligence, earnings tracking, and evolving views.

---

## Structure

Each company page follows a consistent framework:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Thesis | The core investment case in 2–3 sentences |
| Business quality | Moat, ROCE, capital allocation, management |
| Financials | Revenue, margins, balance sheet, cash flow trends |
| Valuation | DCF, comps, implied expectations |
| Risks | What breaks the thesis |
| Catalysts | What accelerates the thesis |
| Verdict | Current stance and conviction level |
