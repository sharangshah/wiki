# Geopolitics

Power dynamics, trade flows, civilisational patterns — the forces that shape everything else.

This domain tracks the macro-political landscape through a structural lens: not who said what today, but which power shifts, trade realignments, and institutional changes will matter in five years.

---

## Key Areas

Great power competition and its implications for capital flows. India's positioning in the emerging multipolar order. Energy security and the geopolitics of commodities. Technology as a geopolitical instrument. Historical patterns that rhyme.
